Frequently Asked Questions

Is NTSU GOODS delivery free?
Yes
How can i cancel my order?
If you haven’t paid for your order yet, you don't need to take further action. Unpaid orders will not be processed by us. If you have already made the payment, call our Customer Support and they will arrange a refund immediately.
Do you accept PayPal?
Yes we accept PayPal
A product i purchased is faulty?
If a product is faulty or damaged, contact our support team within 14 days and we will replace the item or refund.
Can i order batch orders?
We can supply large quantities of individual items but we don't supply batches.
Do i need ID for alcoholic drinks?
Yes providing ID is UK law for all alcoholic drinks and cigarettes for sale
Can i get delivery anywhere?
We only deliver to NTU Clifton Campus halls 'Peverell hall' and 'New hall'
Do i require my student card upon purhcase?
Your student card is not required with purchase as it is attached to your NTSU GOODS account
